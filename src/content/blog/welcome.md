This is my personal blog. I work in data science and polling at [Vantage Data House](https://www.vantagedatahouse.com), and this is where I plan to write about things I find interesting or useful in that space.

## What to expect

I plan to write here about:

- **Data science and methodology** — practical notes on techniques I use in my work
- **Polling and public opinion** — reflections on measurement, estimation, and interpretation
- **Tools and workflows** — things that make my day-to-day work easier

My academic work — publications, working papers, and teaching history — is available under the [Research](/academia/research/) and [Teaching](/academia/teaching/) sections.

More soon.
